# Meta Ads Benchmarks by Niche

Industry-specific performance benchmarks for Meta advertising. Use these to set expectations, evaluate performance, and calibrate test kill criteria.

**Important:** Benchmarks are averages and ranges. Individual account performance depends on offer strength, creative quality, audience size, competition, and seasonality. Use as directional guidance, not absolute targets.

## Seasonal Multipliers

Performance shifts predictably through the year. Plan creative and budget accordingly.

| Period | CPM Impact | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Jan-Feb | -15-25% | Post-holiday drop. Good time to test. |
| Mar-Apr | Baseline | Stable period. |
| May-Jun | +5-10% | Summer spending picks up. |
| Jul-Aug | +5-10% | Back-to-school ramp begins. |
| Sep-Oct | +10-20% | Q4 ramp. Secure creative early. |
| Nov (BFCM) | +30-80% | Highest CPMs of the year. Plan ahead. |
| Dec | +20-40% | Holiday shopping. Drops sharply Dec 26. |

## Notes

- Benchmarks sourced from aggregated industry data and common performance ranges
- Update these as the agent collects real client data over time
- Always compare client performance to their own historical data first, then industry benchmarks
- Benchmarks don't account for iOS privacy changes — actual attribution may be lower than reported
